## 📌 Project Overview
**Uyatsa** is a dedicated digital ecosystem designed to bridge the gap between university students and graduates. Developed under **Sericsoft Innovations Ltd**, the platform serves as a central hub for the Ulumbi Youth and Tertiary Students Association, fostering mentorship, resource sharing, and professional networking to ease the transition from academia to the professional world.

## 🚀 Key Features
* **Member Directory:** A searchable database connecting current tertiary students with established graduates for mentorship.
* **Resource Hub:** A centralized area for sharing academic materials, career advice, and internship opportunities.
* **Community Forums:** Structured discussion areas for various tertiary departments and professional interests.
* **Event Coordination:** Tools for organizing association meetings, career workshops, and community outreach programs.
* **Verified Profiles:** Role-based access ensuring that members are categorized correctly as students or alumni/graduates.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Architecture
Uyatsa utilizes the robust Sericsoft enterprise stack to ensure scalability and ease of use:
* **Frontend:** HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap 5 (Mobile-responsive for students on the go).
* **Backend:** PHP 8+ (Handling complex user associations and data routing).
* **Database:** MySQL 8+ (Optimized for member networking and historical data).
* **Environment:** Fully deployed and optimized for live web production.
